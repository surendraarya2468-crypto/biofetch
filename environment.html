How This Hub Helps Researchers

This page connects environmental problems with useful biological data. A student or researcher can quickly explore which genes, proteins, microbes and pathways are involved in heavy metal resistance, bioremediation, acid mine drainage, biomining and bioenergy. Instead of searching many databases separately, BioFetch gives a focused starting point with direct links to UniProt, NCBI, KEGG, PubMed and AlphaFold.
